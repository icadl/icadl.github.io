ABOUT
AP-iConference 2026
 
Conference Theme is: AI-Empowered Information Science: Bridging Disciplines, Transforming Futures

CALL FOR PAPERS

The AP-iConference 2026 is the Asia-Pacific regional conference of the iSchools organization, open to all information scholars, researchers, and practitioners worldwide. It serves as a premier venue for cutting-edge research in information science, data science, and AI-driven innovation.

The conference theme, “AI-Empowered Information Science: Bridging Disciplines, Transforming Futures,” highlights the transformative role of artificial intelligence in reshaping information ecosystems, bridging disciplinary boundaries, and driving sustainable futures.

We welcome submissions from all areas of information science.

iSchools affiliation is not required.

ON-SITE PROGRAM

4 December 2026
Note: The conference is planned as an on-site event, with online participation options to be arranged depending on the situation.

TOPICS

  • AI and Data Science in Digital Libraries, Archives, and Preservation
  • Human-Information Interaction and AI-Enhanced User Experience
  • Knowledge Discovery and Organization
  • Data & Information Policy, Ethics, and Governance
  • Scholarly Communication, Bibliometrics, and Research Evaluation
  • AI Applications in Education, Healthcare, Cultural Heritage, and beyond
  • Information Behavior and Social Informatics
  • Interdisciplinary and Emerging Topics in Information Science

SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

  • Short Papers：6–8 pages + references
  • Student Symposium：Abstract + references

All submissions must be in English, in PDF format. Papers should follow Springer Computer Science Proceedings guidelines.

All submissions will undergo a double-blind peer review process.

Submission system: EasyChair (link to be announced)
