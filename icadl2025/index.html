Call for Papers: ICADL 2025

Deadline for submission: July 14, 2025

The 27th International Conference on Asia-Pacific Digital Libraries (ICADL 2025)

Dates: December 3 - 5, 2025

Location: Bonifacio Global City, Metro Manila, Philippines

Theme: Intelligence and Equity: Shaping the Future of Knowledge

About the Conference

The International Conference on Asia-Pacific Digital Libraries (ICADL), established in 1998 in Hong Kong, is a premier venue connecting digital library, computer science, and library and information science communities. ICADL 2025 will be hosted by the University of the Philippines School of Library and Information Studies and held at the UP BGC campus in Metro Manila.

This year, ICADL will be co-located with A-LIEP 2025 and the AP-iSchools meeting under the umbrella “2025 International Forum on Data, Information, and Knowledge for Sustainable and Ethical Societies” (IFDIK 2025).

Topics of Interest

  • Human-Centered AI and Ethical AI Development
  • User Studies and Information Behavior
  • Knowledge Infrastructures and Long-Term Access
  • Equity and Inclusion in Knowledge Access
  • Metadata, Ontologies, and Knowledge Organization
  • HCI in Knowledge Environments
  • AI-Powered and Algorithmic Knowledge Discovery
  • Digital Archives and Cultural Heritage Preservation
  • Data Governance, Privacy, and Trust
  • Open Science, Open Data, and Collaborative Networks

Submission Categories

  • Full Papers (12–14 pages + references)
  • Short Papers (6–8 pages + references)
  • Practice Papers (6–8 pages + references)
  • Demo/Poster Papers (4–6 pages + references)
