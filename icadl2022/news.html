Hot News for ICADL 2022

I. Question and answer set on information to attend "The 24th International Conference on Asia-Pacific Digital Libraries (ICADL2022)"

  1. Does the organizer apply for a visa for attendees of the ICADL seminar?

    Firstly, visa applications must be done by attendees themselves.

    Secondly, according to the regulations for Organizing International Conferences in Vietnam, we have to submit data, including attendants' passport scans to The Consulate so that they can verify the information.

    That is the reason why we require attendees to provide us with this information.

  2. I am the author, can I present my article online via Zoom.us?

    The authors are required to physically show up to present their papers at the conference. Otherwise, that author's paper will be automatically rejected in ICADL 2022.

  9. Do I need to quarantine when I come to Vietnam to attend? Or bring any verification documents?

    The Ministry of Health's most recent dispatch states that immigrants entering Vietnam won't be quarantined.

    Requirements for people entering regarding the prevention and control of epidemics:

    For travelers arriving by air, a negative SARS-CoV-2 test result must be obtained prior to departure (with the exception of children under the age of 2), within 72 hours for tests using the RT-PCR/RT method-LAMP or within 24 hours for tests using the rapid antigen test method for the SARS-CoV-2 virus, both of which must be approved by the testing nation's competent authority.

    If the above-mentioned SARS-CoV-2 test results are not negative, a SARS-CoV-2 test must be carried out within the first 24 hours (either by the RT-PCR/RT-LAMP method or by the RT-PCR/RT-LAMP method). SARS-CoV-2 virus fast antigen test) since entrance.

    If the test is positive, the local health authority must be notified right away so that instructions can be given on how to proceed in a timely manner.

    Before entering, a medical declaration must be made.

    People entering Vietnam must utilize the medical declaration application (PC-COVID) as directed during their stay and submit a medical declaration prior to admission.

    If you exhibit SARS-CoV-2 infection symptoms at the border gate (fever, cough, sore throat, runny nose, stuffy nose, body aches, fatigue, chills, decreased or lost taste, decreased or lost sense of smell, pain), immediately notify the health agency at the border gate to take the recommended medical steps.
