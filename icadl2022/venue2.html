Main venue: VNU-LIC Library and Digital knowledge Center,
Vietnam National University, Hanoi

The Library and Digital knowledge Center- an ideal place for eco-friendly green conferences, consists of two main leaf-shaped buildings: the library building, which has an area of
200m2 for students, lecturers, and scientists, and the operation building, which has an area of 200m2 for the Center's managers and staff. The project also includes conference
rooms, as well as facilities and services such as a coffee shop and a supermarket where everyone can relax, read books, and go shopping. In addition to 400m2 of indoor floor
space, the surrounding landscape includes more than 2000m2 of outdoor lawn space that can accommodate 500 people at once.

The Library and Digital knowledge Center will be designed with 4 large meeting halls for ICADL 2022: 2 indoor meeting rooms and 2 outdoor meeting rooms, with a total capacity of up to 400 people. This will be an ideal location for a one-of-a-kind conference, an opportunity for researchers to come and conduct research while enjoying the fresh air that nature offers us.

Address: VNU-LIC Library and Digital knowledge Center, Vietnam National University, Hanoi- Thach Hoa Commune, Thach That District, Hanoi City
